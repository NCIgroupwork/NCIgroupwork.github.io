How do I book in to fitness classes?

All classes are free. Some classes have limited numbers and require booking in advance. These classes are marked on the timetable with an * in front. These classes can be booked in advance on the day of the class. For swimming classes you can book in person when you arrive at the club. For all other classes just simply turn up 5/10 minutes beforehand and no booking is required.
